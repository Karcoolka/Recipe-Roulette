How to Use Recipe Roulette

Step 1: Authenticate

Sign in by creating your login with your email and a password (minimum 6 characters). Then log in to access your account.

Step 2: Fetch Data

Click on Manage -> Fetch Data to download a selection of recipes from the database to your account.

Allowe only for Admin: Click on Manage -> Save Data to save new data into the DB.

Step 3: Manage Recipes

  • Each recipe includes its own ingredients.
  • You can add new recipes with ingredients and their quantities.
  • Edit or delete any recipe or ingredient as needed.

Step 4: Generate a Shopping List

To create a shopping list for your recipes:

  • Select a recipe.
  • Click Manage Recipe -> To Shopping List.
  • All ingredients and their quantities will be added to your shopping list.

Step 5: Logout/Login

You can still see your fetched/added/edited Recipes and yourShopping list full of your customized ingredients

Enjoy cooking with Recipe Roulette!
